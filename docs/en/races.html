📘 Common Species (PHB 2024)

🧑 Human Common (PHB 2024)
Resourceful | Skillful

The world's most adaptable people — ambitious, resourceful, and found everywhere.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Resourceful: At the end of each Long Rest, you gain Heroic Inspiration.
  • Skillful: Proficiency in one skill of your choice.
  • Versatile: One Origin feat of your choice.
🧝 Elf Common (PHB 2024)
Darkvision | Fey Ancestry

A graceful, long-lived people of fey origin.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saving throws against the Charmed condition.
  • Keen Senses: Proficiency in one skill: Insight, Perception, or Survival.
  • Trance: You don't sleep; your Long Rest is 4 hours of meditation.
  • Elven Lineage: Choose a lineage below — it grants spells (spellcasting ability: Int/Wis/Cha).

Subraces

High Elf

  • Cantrip: You know Prestidigitation; on a Long Rest you can replace it with another Wizard cantrip.
  • Lineage Spells: At 3rd level — Detect Magic; at 5th — Misty Step.

Wood Elf

  • Fleet of Foot: Speed 35 feet.
  • Lineage Spells: You know Druidcraft; at 3rd — Longstrider; at 5th — Pass without Trace.

Drow

  • Improved Darkvision: Range 120 feet.
  • Lineage Spells: You know Dancing Lights; at 3rd — Faerie Fire; at 5th — Darkness.
🧔 Dwarf Common (PHB 2024)
Darkvision | Dwarven Resilience

A hardy people of mountains and underground strongholds, masters of stone.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 120 feet.
  • Dwarven Resilience: Resistance to Poison damage and advantage on saving throws against the Poisoned condition.
  • Dwarven Toughness: Your Hit Point maximum increases by 1, and by 1 again every time you gain a level.
  • Stonecunning: As a Bonus Action, gain Tremorsense 60 ft. for 10 minutes. Uses = Proficiency Bonus.
🧒 Halfling Common (PHB 2024)
Brave | Halfling Nimbleness

A small, cheerful people who value home and peace.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Small
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Brave: Advantage on saving throws against the Frightened condition.
  • Halfling Nimbleness: You can move through the space of any creature larger than you.
  • Lucky: When you roll a 1 on the d20 of a D20 Test, you can reroll the die.
  • Naturally Stealthy: You can hide even when obscured only by a creature larger than you.

Small, inventive busybodies with a love of mechanisms.

Core Traits:

  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Gnomish Cunning: Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.

Subraces

Rock Gnome

🐉 Dragonborn Common (PHB 2024)
Draconic Ancestry | Darkvision

Proud descendants of dragons whose breath reflects their lineage.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Draconic Ancestry: Choose a dragon type — it determines your damage type (Acid/Lightning/Fire/Poison/Cold).
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Breath Weapon: In place of an attack: a 15-ft. cone or 30-ft. line, Dexterity save (DC 8 + Con modifier + Proficiency Bonus), 1d10 damage (rising to 2d10/3d10/4d10 at levels 5/11/17). Uses = Proficiency Bonus.
  • Damage Resistance: Resistance to the damage type of your ancestry.
  • Draconic Flight: At 5th level, as a Bonus Action — wings: a Fly Speed equal to your Speed for 10 minutes. Once per Long Rest.

👹 Orc Common (PHB 2024)
Adrenaline Rush | Darkvision

Strong, tireless descendants of Gruumsh, built for endurance.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Adrenaline Rush: Take the Dash action as a Bonus Action and gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus. Regained on a Short or Long Rest.
  • Darkvision: Range 120 feet.
  • Relentless Endurance: When reduced to 0 Hit Points but not killed, you drop to 1 Hit Point instead. Once per Long Rest.

Darkvision | Otherworldly Presence

Descendants of an infernal legacy, marked by horns and a tail.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Otherworldly Presence: You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip. Spellcasting ability: Int/Wis/Cha.

Abyssal

😇 Aasimar Common (PHB 2024)
Darkvision | Celestial Resistance

Bearers of a celestial spark, marked by the influence of the higher planes.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Celestial Resistance: Resistance to Necrotic and Radiant damage.
  • Healing Hands: As an action, touch a creature and roll a number of d4s equal to your Proficiency Bonus, restoring that many Hit Points. Once per Long Rest.
  • Light Bearer: You know the Light cantrip (Charisma).
  • Celestial Revelation: At 3rd level choose a form below; as a Bonus Action for 1 minute, add damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus once per turn. Once per Long Rest.

Subraces

Heavenly Wings

  • Revelation: Wings: a Fly Speed equal to your Speed for the duration.

Inner Radiance

  • Revelation: Radiance (10-ft. light); nearby enemies take Radiant damage.

Necrotic Shroud

  • Revelation: Spectral wings frighten nearby enemies; extra Necrotic damage.
🗿 Goliath Common (PHB 2024)
Giant Ancestry | Large Form

A towering highland people with a drop of giant blood.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 35 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Giant Ancestry: Choose a giant gift below. Uses = Proficiency Bonus.
  • Large Form: At 5th level, as a Bonus Action you become Large for 10 minutes (advantage on Strength, +10 ft. Speed). Once per Long Rest.
  • Powerful Build: Advantage against the Grappled condition and you count as one size larger for carrying capacity.

Subraces

Cloud's Jaunt

  • Gift: As a Bonus Action, teleport up to 30 ft.

Fire's Burn

  • Gift: When you hit with an attack, deal an extra 1d10 Fire damage.

Frost's Chill

  • Gift: When you hit with an attack, deal an extra 1d6 Cold damage and reduce the target's Speed by 10 ft.

Hill's Tumble

  • Gift: When you hit with an attack, you can knock the target Prone.

Stone's Endurance

  • Gift: As a Reaction, reduce the damage taken by 1d12 + your Constitution modifier.

Storm's Thunder

  • Gift: As a Reaction when you take damage, deal 1d8 Thunder damage to a creature within range.

🎭 Changeling Eberron
Changeling Instincts | Shapeshifting

Fey shapeshifters who can take on any face. Source: Eberron.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Changeling Instincts: Proficiency in two skills: Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion.

🧠 Kalashtar Eberron
Dual Mind | Mental Discipline

A union of humans and quori spirits from dreams. An Aberration. Source: Eberron.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Dual Mind: Advantage on Wisdom and Charisma saving throws.
  • Mental Discipline: Resistance to Psychic damage.
  • Mind Link: Telepathy within a range of 10 ft. × your level; you can let a creature respond telepathically for 1 hour.
  • Severed from Dreams: You can't be the target of the Sleep spell; after a Long Rest you gain proficiency in one skill of your choice.
🧝‍♂️ Khoravar Eberron
Darkvision | Fey Ancestry

Descendants of the humans and elves of Khorvaire ("children of Khorvaire"). Eberron's equivalent of the half-elf. Source: Eberron.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saving throws against the Charmed condition.
  • Fey Gift: You know the Friends cantrip; on a Long Rest you can replace it with a Cleric, Druid, or Wizard cantrip.
  • Sleepless Resilience: You can treat a failed save against the Unconscious condition as a success. You can do so again after 1d4 Long Rests.
  • Skill Versatility: Proficiency in one skill or tool; you can change it on a Long Rest.
🐺 Shifter Eberron
Bestial Instincts | Darkvision

Descendants of lycanthropes who can temporarily heighten their bestial traits. Source: Eberron.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Bestial Instincts: Proficiency in one skill: Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, or Survival.
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Shifting: As a Bonus Action for 1 minute, take on a bestial form; gain Temporary Hit Points equal to 2 × your Proficiency Bonus. Uses = Proficiency Bonus. Choose a type below.

Subraces

Beasthide

  • Shifting: +1d6 Temporary Hit Points and +1 AC while shifted.

Longtooth

  • Shifting: As a Bonus Action, make a fang Unarmed Strike: 1d6 + your Strength modifier Piercing damage.

Swiftstride

  • Shifting: +10 ft. Speed; as a Reaction, move up to 10 ft. when an enemy ends its turn near you.

Wildhunt

  • Shifting: Advantage on Wisdom checks; enemies don't have advantage on attacks against you.
🤖 Warforged Eberron
Constructed Resilience | Integrated Protection

Sentient constructs of wood and metal, created for the Last War. Source: Eberron.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 30 feet
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Constructed Resilience: Resistance to Poison damage and advantage on saving throws against the Poisoned condition.
  • Integrated Protection: +1 AC; armor you wear can't be removed against your will.
  • Sentry's Rest: You don't sleep; your Long Rest is 6 hours spent motionless but conscious. Magic can't put you to sleep.
  • Specialized Design: Proficiency in one skill (your choice) and one tool set.
  • Tireless: You don't gain levels of Exhaustion from thirst, hunger, or suffocation.

🧛 Dhampir Exotic
Darkvision | Spider Climb

Living people with vampiric abilities, cursed with a macabre hunger. Source: Van Richten's Guide to Ravenloft.

Core Traits:

  • Size: Medium or Small (your choice)
  • Speed: 35 feet (climb 35 ft.)
  • Languages: Common + 2 languages from your background
  • Darkvision: Range 60 feet.
  • Spider Climb: Climb Speed equal to your Speed. At 3rd level, you can move along vertical surfaces and ceilings while leaving your hands free.
  • Deathless Nature: Resistance to Necrotic damage.
  • Vampiric Bite: As an Unarmed Strike, bite: 1d4 + your Constitution modifier Piercing damage. When you deal damage (not to a Construct or Undead), either restore that many Hit Points or gain a bonus to your next check/attack. Uses = Proficiency Bonus.

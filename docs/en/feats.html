Feats

A complete list of feats, fighting styles, and epic boons (2024 rules).

🔍
Artificer Initiate Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
1 cantrip and spell from the artificer list, tool proficiency

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Magical Training. You learn one cantrip and one 1st-level spell from the artificer spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
  • Free Use. You can cast this 1st-level spell once without expending a spell slot (recharges after a long rest).
  • Tool Master. You gain proficiency with one type of artisan's tools of your choice.
Charger Player's Handbook
Bonus attack or shove after a Dash

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

If you use the Dash action, you can make one melee weapon attack or shove a creature as a bonus action.

  • Powerful Blow. If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line before the attack, you add +5 to the damage or push the target 10 feet away from you.
Chef Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
+1 Con/Wis, cook food for healing and temporary HP

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution or Wisdom by 1.
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with cook's utensils.
  • Nourishing Food. During a short rest, you prepare food that lets allies regain an extra 1d8 HP.
  • Treats. You can prepare treats that grant temporary HP equal to your proficiency bonus.
Crossbow Expert Player's Handbook
Ignore loading, point-blank shots, bonus attack with a hand crossbow

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Rapid Reload. You ignore the loading property of crossbows you're proficient with.
  • Close Quarters. Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attacks.
  • Double Shot. If you attack with a one-handed weapon, you can use a bonus action to attack with a hand crossbow.
Crusher Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
+1 Str/Con, move enemy on hit, advantage on crit

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Constitution by 1.
  • Shove. Once per turn when you deal bludgeoning damage, you can move the creature 5 feet to an unoccupied space.
  • Staggering Crit. On a critical hit with bludgeoning damage, all attacks against that creature have advantage until the end of your next turn.
Defensive Duelist Player's Handbook
Proficiency bonus to AC as a reaction

General feat (Prerequisite: Dexterity 13+).

When you're wielding a finesse weapon you're proficient with and another creature hits you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction.

  • Parry. Add your proficiency bonus to your AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss.
Dragon Fear Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Str/Con/Cha, replace your breath with a frightful roar

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Dragonborn).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength, Constitution, or Charisma by 1.
  • Frightful Roar. Instead of using your Breath Weapon, you can let out a roar. Each creature of your choice within 30 feet must make a Wisdom saving throw or be Frightened for 1 minute.
Dragon Hide Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Str/Con/Cha, AC 13+Dex without armor, claws

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Dragonborn).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength, Constitution, or Charisma by 1.
  • Scaled Skin. Your skin hardens. While you wear no armor, your AC equals 13 + your Dexterity modifier.
  • Claws. You grow claws. Your unarmed strikes deal 1d4 + your Strength modifier slashing damage.
Drow High Magic Xanathar's Guide to Everything
Detect Magic (unlimited), Levitate and Dispel Magic

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Elf (Drow)).

You master the ancient magic of your people. You learn the following spells:

  • Detect Magic. You can cast it an unlimited number of times without expending spell slots.
  • Levitate and Dispel Magic. You can cast each of these spells once (recharges after a long rest).
Dual Wielder Player's Handbook
+1 AC, use non-light weapons, quick draw

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Two-Weapon Defense. You gain +1 AC while wielding a separate melee weapon in each hand.
  • Heavy Pairing. You can use two-weapon fighting even if the weapons aren't "light" (but they must still be one-handed).
  • Quick Draw. You can draw or stow two one-handed weapons instead of one.
Dungeon Delver Player's Handbook
Advantage to find traps, resistance to their damage

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Seeker's Eye. Advantage on Perception and Investigation checks to find hidden doors and traps.
  • Trap Resilience. Advantage on saving throws to avoid traps and resistance to the damage they deal.
  • Quick Search. You can search for traps while moving at your normal speed.
Durable Player's Handbook
+1 Con, more effective Hit Dice healing

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution by 1.
  • Tough Health. When you roll a Hit Die to regain HP, the minimum amount you regain per die equals twice your Constitution modifier.
Dwarf Fortitude Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Con, healing while Dodging

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Dwarf).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution by 1.
  • Combat Recovery. Whenever you use the Dodge action in combat, you can spend one Hit Die to regain hit points.
Eldritch Adept Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
Gain one Eldritch Invocation

General feat (Prerequisite: Spellcasting ability).

  • Eldritch Invocation. You learn one Eldritch Invocation of your choice from the Warlock list. If the invocation has prerequisites, you must meet them.
  • Flexibility. Whenever you gain a level, you can replace this invocation with another.
Elemental Adept Player's Handbook
Ignore elemental resistance, minimum 2 damage on dice

General feat (Prerequisite: Ability to cast at least one spell).

  • Resistance Breaker. Choose a damage type: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. Your spells ignore resistance to that damage type.
  • Stable Damage. When you roll damage dice for a spell of the chosen type, any "1" on a die counts as a "2".
Elven Accuracy Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Dex/Int/Wis/Cha, reroll a die when you have advantage

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Elf or Half-Elf).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1 (to a maximum of 20).
  • Extraordinary Accuracy. When you have advantage on an attack roll using Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma, you can reroll one of the advantage dice.
Fighting Initiate Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
Gain one Fighter Fighting Style

General feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with a martial weapon).

  • Fighting Style. You choose one Fighting Style option from the Fighter class list.
  • Style Change. Whenever you gain a level that grants an ability score improvement, you can replace this style with another.
Flames of Phlegethos Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Int/Cha, reroll fire damage, fire aura

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Tiefling).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence or Charisma by 1.
  • Hellish Flames. When you roll fire damage for a spell, you can reroll any "1" on the dice.
  • Aura of Fire. When you cast a fire spell, you wreathe yourself in flames until the end of your next turn. It sheds light and deals 1d4 fire damage to anyone who hits you with a melee attack.
Gift of the Chromatic Dragon Fizban's Treasury of Dragons
Extra elemental weapon damage, reactive resistance

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Weapon Infusion. As a bonus action, you can infuse a weapon with elemental energy (acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison) for 1 minute. The weapon deals an extra 1d4 damage of the chosen type.
  • Reactive Resistance. When you take elemental damage, you can use your reaction to gain resistance to that attack.
Gift of the Gem Dragon Fizban's Treasury of Dragons
+1 Int/Wis/Cha, telekinetic repulsion

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1.
  • Telekinetic Reprisal. When a creature within 10 feet deals damage to you, you can use your reaction to force it to make a Strength saving throw. On a failure, it takes 2d8 force damage and is pushed 10 feet away.
Gift of the Metallic Dragon Fizban's Treasury of Dragons
Healing spell, protective wings (bonus to AC)

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Draconic Healing. You learn the spell Cure Wounds. You can cast it for free once per long rest.
  • Protective Wings. When you or a creature near you is hit, you can use your reaction to manifest spectral wings, adding your proficiency bonus to AC against that attack.
Grappler Player's Handbook
Advantage on attacks against a grappled target, ability to pin the enemy

General feat (Prerequisite: Strength 13+).

  • Grappler's Advantage. You have advantage on attack rolls against a creature you are grappling.
  • Pin. You can try to pin a creature. Make another grapple check; on a success, you both become Restrained until the grapple ends.
Great Weapon Master Player's Handbook
Bonus attack on a crit/kill, -5 to attack for +10 damage

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Cleave. When you score a critical hit or drop a creature to 0 HP with a heavy weapon, you can make one melee attack as a bonus action.
  • Powerful Attack. Before attacking with a heavy weapon, you can take a -5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, add +10 to the damage.
Gunner Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
+1 Dex, firearm proficiency, point-blank shooting

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Dexterity by 1.
  • Weapon Mastery. You gain proficiency with firearms and ignore their loading property.
  • Close Quarters. An enemy being within 5 feet doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attacks.
Healer Player's Handbook
Stabilize with 1 HP, healing with a healer's kit

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • First Aid. When you stabilize a creature with a healer's kit, it also regains 1 HP.
  • Field Medicine. As an action, you can use a healer's kit to heal a creature for 1d6 + 4 + its number of Hit Dice. The creature can't regain HP this way again until it finishes a short rest.
Heavily Armored Player's Handbook
+1 Str, heavy armor proficiency

General feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength by 1.
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with heavy armor.
Heavy Armor Master Player's Handbook
+1 Str, -3 physical damage in heavy armor

General feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with heavy armor).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength by 1.
  • Armored Resilience. While wearing heavy armor, nonmagical bludgeoning, slashing, and piercing damage you take is reduced by 3.
Infernal Constitution Xanathar's Guide to Everything
+1 Con, resistance to cold and poison

Racial feat (Prerequisite: Tiefling).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution by 1.
  • Blood Resilience. You gain resistance to cold and poison, and advantage on saving throws against being poisoned.
Inspiring Leader Player's Handbook
Grant temporary HP to allies with a speech

General feat (Prerequisite: Charisma 13+).

  • Inspiring Speech. By spending 10 minutes, you can inspire up to 6 friendly creatures (including yourself). Each gains temporary HP equal to your level + your Charisma modifier.
Keen Mind Player's Handbook
+1 Int, perfect memory, sense of time/space

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence by 1.
  • Analytical Memory. You always know which way is north and how many hours are left before sunset, and you can accurately recall anything you've seen or heard within the past month.
Lightly Armored Player's Handbook
+1 Str/Dex, light armor proficiency

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1 (to a maximum of 20).
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with light armor.
Lucky Player's Handbook
3 luck points to reroll dice

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Luck Points. You gain 3 luck points. You can spend one to roll an additional d20 for an attack, ability check, or saving throw, and choose which die to use.
  • Influence the Enemy. You can also spend a point to force an enemy to reroll an attack roll against you. Points are regained after a long rest.
Mage Slayer Player's Handbook
Reaction attack against a spellcaster, disadvantage on enemy concentration

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Spell Disruption. When a creature within 5 feet casts a spell, you can use your reaction to attack it.
  • Concentration. When you deal damage to a creature concentrating on a spell, it has disadvantage on the saving throw to maintain concentration.
  • Magic Resistance. You have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by creatures within 5 feet of you.
Martial Adept Player's Handbook
2 fighter maneuvers, 1 superiority die (d6)

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Combat Maneuvers. You learn two maneuvers from the fighter's Battle Master archetype.
  • Superiority Die. You gain one d6 superiority die (if you already have such dice, add one more to your pool). It is regained after a short or long rest.
Medium Armor Master Player's Handbook
Max Dexterity bonus +3, no disadvantage on Stealth

General feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor).

  • Improved Agility. While wearing medium armor, you can add up to +3 of your Dexterity modifier to your AC instead of +2.
  • Quiet Movement. You don't have disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks from wearing medium armor.
Metamagic Adept Tasha's Cauldron of Everything
2 metamagic options, 2 sorcery points

General feat (Prerequisite: The ability to cast a spell).

  • Metamagic. You learn two Metamagic options from the sorcerer class list. You can change them when you gain a level.
  • Sorcery Points. You gain 2 sorcery points to use for metamagic (they are regained after a long rest).
Mobile Player's Handbook
+10 speed, ignore difficult terrain when Dashing

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Speed. Your movement speed increases by 10 feet.
  • Escape without Provoking. When you make a melee attack against a creature, it can't make opportunity attacks against you for the rest of your turn, whether you hit or not.
  • Nimble Dash. When you take the Dash action, difficult terrain doesn't cost you extra movement.

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Protect the Mount. You can force an attack aimed at your mount to target you instead.
  • Evasion. If your mount is subjected to an effect that allows a Dexterity save for half damage, it takes no damage on a success and only half on a failure.
Observant Player's Handbook

Piercer Tasha's Cauldron of Everything

Polearm Master Player's Handbook

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

Stop an enemy with an opportunity attack, protect allies

General feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Sentinel. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from you even if they take the Disengage action.
  • Avenge an Ally. When a creature within 5 feet attacks someone other than you (who doesn't have this feat), you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against that creature.

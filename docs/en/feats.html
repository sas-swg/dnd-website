Feats

A complete list of feats, fighting styles, and epic boons (2024 rules).

🔍

Origin Feats (10)

Granted by your background at character creation.

Alert Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Add Proficiency Bonus to Initiative; swap Initiative with an ally

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Initiative Proficiency. Add your Proficiency Bonus to your Initiative rolls.
  • Initiative Swap. You can swap your Initiative with a willing ally, provided neither of you is Incapacitated.
Crafter Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Discounts, tool proficiency, faster crafting

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Tool Proficiency. Gain proficiency with three Artisan's Tools of your choice.
  • Discount. Whenever you buy a nonmagical item, you get a 20 percent discount.
  • Fast Crafting. During a Long Rest you can craft one piece of gear from a set list if you have the right tools.
Healer Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Stabilize with 1 HP, healing with a healer's kit

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • First Aid. When you stabilize a creature with a healer's kit, it also regains 1 HP.
  • Field Medicine. As an action, you can use a healer's kit to heal a creature for 1d6 + 4 + its number of Hit Dice. The creature can't regain HP this way again until it finishes a short rest.
Lucky Origin Feat • PHB 2024
3 luck points to reroll dice

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Luck Points. You gain 3 luck points. You can spend one to roll an additional d20 for an attack, ability check, or saving throw, and choose which die to use.
  • Influence the Enemy. You can also spend a point to force an enemy to reroll an attack roll against you. Points are regained after a long rest.
Magic Initiate Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Two cantrips + one 1st-level spell (Cleric/Druid/Wizard)

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Cantrips. Learn two cantrips from the Cleric, Druid, or Wizard spell list.
  • 1st-Level Spell. Learn one 1st-level spell; cast it once per Long Rest for free, or with spell slots.
  • Spellcasting Ability. Choose Intelligence (Wizard) or Wisdom (Cleric/Druid).
  • Repeatable. You can take this feat more than once, choosing a different list each time.
Musician Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Musical instrument proficiency; grant Heroic Inspiration

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Instruments. Gain proficiency with three Musical Instruments of your choice.
  • Encouragement. After a Rest spent playing, grant Heroic Inspiration to allies equal to your Proficiency Bonus.
Savage Attacker Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Reroll melee weapon damage dice

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Reroll Damage. Once per turn when you hit with a melee attack, you can reroll the weapon's damage dice and choose either result.
Skilled Origin Feat • PHB 2024
Proficiency in 3 skills or tools

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Versatility. You gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice.
Tavern Brawler Origin Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Con, improvised weapon proficiency, grapple as a bonus action

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Constitution by 1.
  • Street Fighter. You gain proficiency with improvised weapons, and your unarmed strikes deal 1d4 damage.
  • Quick Grapple. When you hit with an unarmed strike or improvised weapon, you can use a bonus action to try to grapple the target.
Tough Origin Feat • PHB 2024
+2 HP per level

Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Health Reserve. Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your level.
  • Further Growth. Whenever you gain a level after taking this feat, your hit point maximum increases by an additional 2.

General Feats (42)

Available from level 4 in place of an Ability Score Improvement.

Ability Score Improvement General Feat • PHB 2024
+2 to one ability or +1 to two (max 20)

General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+).

  • Improvement. Increase one ability score by 2, or two ability scores by 1 each, to a maximum of 20.
  • Repeatable. You can take this feat more than once.
Actor General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Charisma; advantage on impersonation

General Feat (Prerequisite: Charisma 13+).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Impersonation. Advantage on Deception and Performance checks to pass yourself off as someone else.
  • Mimicry. You can mimic the speech or voice of a creature you've heard for at least 1 minute.
Athlete General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex; quick standing and climbing

General Feat (Prerequisite: Strength or Dexterity 13+).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Stand Up. Standing up from Prone uses only 5 feet of movement.
  • Climbing. Climbing doesn't cost you extra movement.
  • Jump. You need only 5 feet of movement, not 10, before a running jump.
Charger General Feat • PHB 2024
Bonus attack or shove after a Dash

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Powerful Blow. If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line before the attack, you add +5 to the damage or push the target 10 feet away from you.
Chef General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Con/Wis, cook food for healing and temporary HP

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution or Wisdom by 1.
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with cook's utensils.
  • Nourishing Food. During a short rest, you prepare food that lets allies regain an extra 1d8 HP.
  • Treats. You can prepare treats that grant temporary HP equal to your proficiency bonus.
Crossbow Expert General Feat • PHB 2024
Ignore loading, point-blank shots, bonus attack with a hand crossbow

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Rapid Reload. You ignore the loading property of crossbows you're proficient with.
  • Close Quarters. Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attacks.
  • Double Shot. If you attack with a one-handed weapon, you can use a bonus action to attack with a hand crossbow.
Crusher General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Con, move enemy on hit, advantage on crit

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Constitution by 1.
  • Shove. Once per turn when you deal bludgeoning damage, you can move the creature 5 feet to an unoccupied space.
  • Staggering Crit. On a critical hit with bludgeoning damage, all attacks against that creature have advantage until the end of your next turn.
Defensive Duelist General Feat • PHB 2024
Proficiency bonus to AC as a reaction

General Feat (Prerequisite: Dexterity 13+).

  • Parry. Add your proficiency bonus to your AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss.
Dual Wielder General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 AC, use non-light weapons, quick draw

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Two-Weapon Defense. You gain +1 AC while wielding a separate melee weapon in each hand.
  • Heavy Pairing. You can use two-weapon fighting even if the weapons aren't "light" (but they must still be one-handed).
  • Quick Draw. You can draw or stow two one-handed weapons instead of one.
Durable General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Con, more effective Hit Dice healing

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Constitution by 1.
  • Tough Health. When you roll a Hit Die to regain HP, the minimum amount you regain per die equals twice your Constitution modifier.
Elemental Adept General Feat • PHB 2024
Ignore elemental resistance, minimum 2 damage on dice

General Feat (Prerequisite: Ability to cast at least one spell).

  • Resistance Breaker. Choose a damage type: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. Your spells ignore resistance to that damage type.
  • Stable Damage. When you roll damage dice for a spell of the chosen type, any "1" on a die counts as a "2".
Fey Touched General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int/Wis/Cha; Misty Step + a Divination/Conjuration spell

General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Spells. You learn Misty Step and one 1st-level Divination or Conjuration spell.
  • Free Casting. Cast each of these spells once per Long Rest without a slot, or with spell slots.
Grappler General Feat • PHB 2024
Advantage on attacks against a grappled target, ability to pin the enemy

General Feat (Prerequisite: Strength 13+).

  • Grappler's Advantage. You have advantage on attack rolls against a creature you are grappling.
  • Pin. You can try to pin a creature. Make another grapple check; on a success, you both become Restrained until the grapple ends.
Great Weapon Master General Feat • PHB 2024
Bonus attack on a crit/kill, -5 to attack for +10 damage

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Cleave. When you score a critical hit or drop a creature to 0 HP with a heavy weapon, you can make one melee attack as a bonus action.
  • Powerful Attack. Before attacking with a heavy weapon, you can take a -5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, add +10 to the damage.
Heavily Armored General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str, heavy armor proficiency

General Feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength by 1.
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with heavy armor.
Heavy Armor Master General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str, -3 physical damage in heavy armor

General Feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with heavy armor).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength by 1.
  • Armored Resilience. While wearing heavy armor, nonmagical bludgeoning, slashing, and piercing damage you take is reduced by 3.
Inspiring Leader General Feat • PHB 2024
Grant temporary HP to allies with a speech

General Feat (Prerequisite: Charisma 13+).

  • Inspiring Speech. By spending 10 minutes, you can inspire up to 6 friendly creatures (including yourself). Each gains temporary HP equal to your level + your Charisma modifier.
Keen Mind General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int, perfect memory, sense of time/space

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence by 1.
  • Analytical Memory. You always know which way is north and how many hours are left before sunset, and you can accurately recall anything you've seen or heard within the past month.
Lightly Armored General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex, light armor proficiency

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1 (to a maximum of 20).
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with light armor.
Mage Slayer General Feat • PHB 2024
Reaction attack against a spellcaster, disadvantage on enemy concentration

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Spell Disruption. When a creature within 5 feet casts a spell, you can use your reaction to attack it.
  • Concentration. When you deal damage to a creature concentrating on a spell, it has disadvantage on the saving throw to maintain concentration.
  • Magic Resistance. You have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by creatures within 5 feet of you.
Martial Weapon Training General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex; proficiency with Martial weapons

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Weapon Proficiency. You gain proficiency with all Martial weapons.
Medium Armor Master General Feat • PHB 2024
Max Dexterity bonus +3, no disadvantage on Stealth

General Feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with medium armor).

  • Improved Agility. While wearing medium armor, you can add up to +3 of your Dexterity modifier to your AC instead of +2.
  • Quiet Movement. You don't have disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks from wearing medium armor.
Moderately Armored General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex, medium armor and shield proficiency

General Feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with light armor).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1.
  • Proficiency. You gain proficiency with medium armor and shields.
Mounted Combatant General Feat • PHB 2024
Advantage on mounted attacks, protect your mount

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Tactical Advantage. You have advantage on melee attacks against creatures smaller than your mount.
  • Protect the Mount. You can force an attack aimed at your mount to target you instead.
  • Evasion. If your mount is subjected to an effect that allows a Dexterity save for half damage, it takes no damage on a success and only half on a failure.
Observant General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int/Wis, lip reading, +5 passive Perception

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence or Wisdom by 1.
  • Lip Reading. You can understand a creature's speech by watching its lips (if you know that language).
  • Keen Sight. You gain a +5 bonus to passive Perception and passive Investigation.
Piercer General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex, reroll piercing damage die

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1.
  • Piercing Strike. Once per turn when you hit with piercing damage, you can reroll one damage die.
  • Lethal Lunge. On a critical hit with a piercing weapon, you add one extra damage die of that weapon.
Poisoner General Feat • PHB 2024
Ignore poison resistance, apply poison as a bonus action

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Poison Master. You ignore resistance to poison damage.
  • Quick Coating. You can apply poison to a weapon as a bonus action instead of an action.
  • Crafting. You gain proficiency with a poisoner's kit and can create potent poison during a long rest.
Polearm Master General Feat • PHB 2024
Bonus attack with the other end, opportunity attack when an enemy approaches

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Extra Strike. When you attack with a halberd, quarterstaff, or spear, you can use a bonus action to strike with the opposite end of the weapon (1d4 damage).
  • Perimeter Defense. You can make an opportunity attack when a creature enters your reach (not only when it leaves).
Resilient General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 to an ability and proficiency in saving throws with that ability

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Hardening. Choose one ability score. Increase it by 1.
  • Saving Throw Proficiency. You gain proficiency in saving throws of the chosen ability.
Ritual Caster General Feat • PHB 2024
Ability to record and cast ritual spells

General Feat (Prerequisite: Intelligence or Wisdom 13+).

  • Ritual Book. You gain a ritual book with two 1st-level spells from a chosen class list.
  • Adding New Ones. If you find a scroll or book with a spell that has the "ritual" tag, you can copy it into your book (2 hours and 50 gp per spell level).
Sentinel General Feat • PHB 2024
Stop an enemy with an opportunity attack, protect allies

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Iron Grip. When you hit with an opportunity attack, the creature's speed drops to 0 for the rest of the turn.
  • Sentinel. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from you even if they take the Disengage action.
  • Avenge an Ally. When a creature within 5 feet attacks someone other than you (who doesn't have this feat), you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against that creature.
Shadow Touched General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int/Wis/Cha, Invisibility spell and one more 1st-level spell

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1.
  • Shadow Magic. You learn the spell Invisibility and one 1st-level spell of your choice from the Illusion or Necromancy schools.
  • Free Use. Each spell can be cast without expending a spell slot once per long rest.
Sharpshooter General Feat • PHB 2024
Ignore cover, -5 to attack for +10 damage on ranged attacks

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Long Range. Long-range weapon attacks don't impose disadvantage.
  • Cover Penetration. Your ranged attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover.
  • Powerful Shot. Before a ranged attack with a weapon you're proficient with, you can take a -5 penalty to the roll; on a hit, add +10 to the damage.
Shield Master General Feat • PHB 2024
Shield shove, add shield bonus to Dexterity saving throws

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Shield Bash. When you attack on your turn, you can use a bonus action to try to shove a creature with your shield.
  • Shield Defense. You add your shield's AC to any Dexterity saving throw against spells that target only you.
  • Evasion. If you must make a Dexterity save for half damage, you can use your reaction to take no damage on a success by hiding behind your shield.
Skill Expert General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 to any ability, 1 skill, 1 expertise

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase any one ability score by 1.
  • Skill. You gain proficiency in one skill.
  • Expertise. Choose one skill you're proficient in; your proficiency bonus is doubled for it.
Skulker General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Dexterity; hide and snipe from cover

General Feat (Prerequisite: Dexterity 13+).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Sniper. Missing with a ranged attack while hidden doesn't reveal your position.
  • Quick Hide. As a Bonus Action you can try to Hide even when only Lightly Obscured.
Slasher General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Str/Dex, slow an enemy, disadvantage to enemy on a crit

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1.
  • Deep Cut. Once per turn when you hit with slashing damage, you can reduce the target's speed by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.
  • Trauma. On a critical hit, you inflict such a wound that the creature has disadvantage on all attack rolls until the end of your next turn.
Speedy General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Dexterity; +10 ft Speed; better Dash

General Feat (Prerequisite: Dexterity 13+).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Speed. Your Speed increases by 10 feet.
  • Nimble Dash. When you Dash, Difficult Terrain doesn't slow you that turn.
  • Hard to Catch. Opportunity Attacks have Disadvantage against you on turns when you Dash.
Spell Sniper General Feat • PHB 2024
Double spell range, ignore cover, 1 cantrip

General Feat (Prerequisite: The ability to cast a spell).

  • Long-Range Magic. The range of spells that require an attack roll is doubled.
  • Accuracy. Your spell attacks ignore half and three-quarters cover.
  • Extra Magic. You learn one cantrip that requires an attack roll.
Telekinetic General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int/Wis/Cha, enhanced Mage Hand, telekinetic shove

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1.
  • Mental Hand. You learn the cantrip Mage Hand. It is invisible and requires no components.
  • Telekinetic Shove. As a bonus action, you can try to shove a creature within 30 feet 5 feet away (Strength save against your spell save DC).
Telepathic General Feat • PHB 2024
+1 Int/Wis/Cha, mental communication, Detect Thoughts spell

General Feat (Prerequisite: None).

  • Ability Score Increase. Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1.
  • Telepathy. You can communicate telepathically with any creature you can see within 60 feet (it must understand at least one language).
  • Read Thoughts. You can cast the spell Detect Thoughts without expending spell slots or components once per long rest.
War Caster General Feat • PHB 2024
Advantage on concentration, cast a spell as an opportunity attack

General Feat (Prerequisite: Ability to cast at least one spell).

  • Combat Concentration. You have advantage on Constitution saving throws to maintain concentration when you take damage.
  • Spellcasting with Full Hands. You can perform the somatic (gesture) components of spells even when you have weapons or a shield in both hands.
  • Reactive Spell. When an enemy's movement provokes an opportunity attack, you can cast a spell instead of attacking (casting time: 1 action, targeting only that creature).

Fighting Styles (10)

Available to classes with the Fighting Style feature.

Archery Fighting Style • PHB 2024
+2 to ranged weapon attack rolls

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Accuracy. You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls with Ranged weapons.
Blind Fighting Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Blindsight 10 feet

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Blindsight. You have Blindsight with a range of 10 feet, sensing everything not behind Total Cover even when Blinded or in darkness.
Defense Fighting Style • PHB 2024
+1 AC while wearing armor

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Defense. While wearing armor, you gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class.
Dueling Fighting Style • PHB 2024
+2 damage with a single one-handed weapon

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Dueling. When wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, you gain +2 to damage rolls with it.
Great Weapon Fighting Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Treat 1s and 2s as 3 on two-handed weapon damage

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Great Weapon. When you roll damage for a melee weapon used with two hands, treat any 1 or 2 on a damage die as a 3.
Interception Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Reduce damage to a nearby ally as a reaction

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Interception. When a creature you can see hits a target within 5 feet of you, use your Reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + your Proficiency Bonus (need a Shield or weapon).
Protection Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Impose Disadvantage on an attack vs a nearby ally (Shield)

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Protection. While holding a Shield, when a creature attacks a target within 5 feet of you, use your Reaction to impose Disadvantage on the attack roll.
Thrown Weapon Fighting Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Quick-draw thrown weapons; +2 damage

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Thrown. You can draw a thrown weapon as part of the attack, and gain +2 to damage with thrown weapons.
Two-Weapon Fighting Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Add ability modifier to off-hand damage

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Two-Weapon. When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack.
Unarmed Fighting Fighting Style • PHB 2024
Stronger unarmed strikes and grapples

Fighting Style (Prerequisite: Fighting Style feature).

  • Strike. Your Unarmed Strikes deal 1d6 Bludgeoning damage (1d8 if you have no weapon or Shield).
  • Grapple. At the start of each of your turns, you deal 1d4 Bludgeoning damage to a creature Grappled by you.
